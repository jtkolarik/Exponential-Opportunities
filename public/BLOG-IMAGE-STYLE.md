# Blog Image Style Guide

## The Style
Warm, soft gouache children's book illustration with gentle expressions, rosy cheeks, and a nurturing feel. Clean composition with minimal background.

## Color Palette
- **Child's clothing:** Coral orange / soft red-orange
- **Adult's clothing:** Sage green / olive green
- **Background:** Soft sage, cream, or warm beige with subtle texture
- **Skin tones:** Warm with rosy cheeks
- **Hair:** Brown tones

## Base Prompt Template

warm cheerful illustration of [SCENE DESCRIPTION], child in soft coral sweater, mother in sage green, profile view facing each other, soft rounded gouache style, rosy cheeks, gentle smiles, warm cream sage background with subtle paper texture, children's book illustration, warm and tender moment --ar 16:9 --style raw --s 100 --v 7


## How to Use

Replace `[SCENE DESCRIPTION]` with content relevant to your blog article:

### Example Scenes by Topic:

**Speech development / communication:**
- "toddler speaking to smiling mother"
- "child excitedly telling mother a story"
- "mother listening attentively to toddler"

**Early intervention / getting help:**
- "mother comforting uncertain toddler"
- "child looking up at encouraging mother"
- "mother and child in reassuring moment"

**Milestones / progress:**
- "toddler proudly showing something to mother"
- "mother celebrating with happy toddler"
- "child and mother sharing joyful moment"

**Activities / play-based learning:**
- "mother and toddler playing together"
- "child and mother reading a book together"
- "toddler and mother engaged in playful activity"

**Concerns / when to seek help:**
- "mother gently encouraging shy toddler"
- "caring mother with thoughtful toddler"
- "mother and child in quiet supportive moment"
